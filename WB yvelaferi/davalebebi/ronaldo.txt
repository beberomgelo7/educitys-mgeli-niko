<p>Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese footballer widely considered one of the greatest of all. <br>
     Born in 1985, he rose to fame at Manchester United, dominating with speed, power, and elite finishing <br>


     He became a global superstar at Real Madrid, breaking goal records and winning multiple Champions League titles and Ballon d’Ors. <br>
       Ronaldo is known for his athleticism, professionalism, and longevity, succeeding in England, Spain, Italy, and internationally with Portugal, <br>
      winning Euro 2016 and the Nations League. Even in his late 30s, he remains a top scorer and global icon through his discipline, marketing impact, and relentless competitive mindset.
